# Soul of Leila

**Soul of Leila** is a luxury women’s resortwear brand focused on silk dresses, silk blouses, kimono sets and timeless summer pieces.

Designed with a quiet luxury approach, Soul of Leila brings together elegant silhouettes, premium fabrics and refined details for women who seek effortless sophistication.

## SS’26 Granada Resort Collection

The **Granada Resort Collection** is inspired by Mediterranean elegance, historic patterns, silk textures and modern resortwear dressing.

The collection includes:

- Luxury silk dresses
- Silk blouses
- Kimono sets
- Resortwear pieces
- Summer occasion wear
- Timeless women’s fashion
